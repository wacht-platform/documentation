---
title: 'Platform Methods'
description: 'Method reference for health, analytics, notifications, invitations, waitlist, and segments.'
---

## Health

`client.health()`:
- `check_health()`
- `check_readiness()`

## Analytics

`client.analytics()`:
- `fetch_stats()` with optional `AnalyticsStatsOptions { from, to }`

## Notifications

`client.notifications()`:
- `create(CreateNotificationRequest)`

## Invitations

`client.invitations()`:
- `fetch_invitations()` with list options
- `invite_user(InviteUserRequest)`
- `delete_invitation(invitation_id)`

## Waitlist

`client.waitlist()`:
- `fetch_waitlist_users()` with list options
- `approve_waitlist_user(waitlist_user_id)`

## Segments

`client.segments()`:
- `list_segments()`
- `create_segment(CreateSegmentRequest)`
- `fetch_segment_data(GetSegmentDataRequest)`
- `update_segment(segment_id, UpdateSegmentRequest)`
- `delete_segment(segment_id)`
- `assign_segment(segment_id, AssignEntityRequest)`
- `remove_segment(segment_id, AssignEntityRequest)`

## Contract Notes

- These calls are deployment-scoped and require backend auth.
- All methods return builders with `.send().await`.
